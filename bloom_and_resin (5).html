Our Story

A flower picked. A memory kept.

I'm Kashika, and I started Bloom & Resin because I believe the most beautiful things in life deserve to last forever. A flower blooms for days — but the feeling it gives you? That should never fade.

Every piece I make begins with a real, living flower — pressed by hand, arranged with care, and sealed in crystal-clear resin so it stays just as beautiful as the day it was picked.

No moulds. No duplicates. Every earring, every tray, every candle stand is entirely one of a kind — because that's what you deserve.
